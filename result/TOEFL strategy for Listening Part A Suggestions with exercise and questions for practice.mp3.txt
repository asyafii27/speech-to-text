Tofu exercise 3. In this exercise, 
listen carefully to the short dialogue and question on the recording, 
and then choose the best answer to the question. 
You should pay particular attention to expressions of suggestion. 

Number 1. I'd like to work out this afternoon. 
Let's go to the gym for a while. 
What does the woman suggest?

Number 2. 
I can't finish the work today. 
I'm too tired. 
Why not finish it tomorrow? 
What does the man suggest?

Number 3. 
It's rather cool in here. 
Let's turn on the heat. 
What does the woman suggest?

Number 4. 
My old car has broken down again. 
Why don't you get a new one? 
What does the man suggest?

Number 5. 
We've got so many books here. 
I can't find the one I want. Let's organize them on the shelves. 
What does the woman suggest?

Number 6. 
These clothes are too small for me. 
Why not let your sister have them? 
What does the man suggest to the woman?

Number 7. 
It's too late to leave tonight. 
Let's put off the trip until tomorrow. 
What does the woman suggest?

Number 8. 
I really need to get going on this term paper. 
Why not plan on staying in the library all day? 
What does the man suggest to the woman?

Number 9. 
There's a really funny program coming on television now. 
Let's watch it then. 
What does the woman suggest?

Number 10. 
It's unbelievable, but I've actually got a little extra money now.
Why not put it in the bank? 
What does the woman suggest to the man?
